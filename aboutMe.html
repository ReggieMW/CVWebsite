About Me

Working in the foreclosure business for a decade strongly inspired in me two goals:

  • To help other people achieve their goals and dreams
  • A desire to create a more equitable world in which everyone has the basic needs to live
In my professional career, I've been very lucky to manage and mentor many people that have gone on to achieve great things. I plan on continuing that support while I work towards achieving my personal goal of making the world a better place. I hope to combine my passion for reading, travel, gaming, and human rights to create software that allows me to share those passions with others.

In my free time I enjoy most of it with my wife and our three dogs. I've always been a voracious reader and student, and read books on what some may find an eclectic mix of subjects, both fiction and non-fiction. I also enjoy playing guitar, video games, and NYT puzzles. I've been fortunate enough to travel to over a dozen foreign countries, including living in Prague, Czech Republic for a year teaching English as a foreign language.
